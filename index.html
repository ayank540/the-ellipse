LOCKDOWN IN INDIA: A CURSE FOR MIGRANT LABOURERS

Its been 52 days since our prime minister narendra modi announced complete lockdown in india . The migrant labourers had no idea at that time what time had stored in their fate and what they’ll be going to suffer. The daily wage workers working far from their homes and running their families became jobless over a night. No money, no food , only they had in their mind was the hope of everything becoming normal soon. But things were going to be worsen ahead.

Fourteen migrant workers CRUSHED under train!

Sixteen migrant workers were mowed down by an empty freight train in Maharashtra’s Aurangabad district in the early hours of Friday. While 14 of them died on the spot, two of them later succumbed to injuries. Prime Minister Narendra Modi tweeted, “Extremely anguished by the loss of lives due to the rail accident in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. Have spoken to Railway Minister Piyush Goyal and he is closely monitoring the situation. All possible assistance required is being provided.”
